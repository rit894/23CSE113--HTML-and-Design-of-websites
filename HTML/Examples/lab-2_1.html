AMRITA UNIVERSITY


Placements at amrita University

The placements for Amrita University Kochi are managed by its placement cell every year. The cell is responsible for preparing students for placements and bringing recruiters to the campus. As per Amrita University Kochi placement report 2025, a total of 150 companies visited the campus for placements, and a total of 800 offers were made. 700 students were placed with an average package of Rs 7 LPA.

These are the biggest contributors to Amrita placements every year.

  • Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)
    • One of India’s largest IT service providers. They offer roles in software development, testing, and IT consulting.
  • Infosys
    • A global IT consulting company known for its Mysore training campus. They hire for system engineer and digital specialist roles.

  • Wipro
    • A major IT services and consulting company offering roles in cloud, cybersecurity, and enterprise IT.

  • Cognizant (CTS)

    • Known for digital transformation and IT services. They typically offer GenC and GenC Pro roles.

  • Tech Mahindra

    • Focuses on telecom, enterprise solutions, and software development.

The highest packages and roles offered by CTS, Tech Mahindra, Wipro, Infosys, and TCS at Amrita University. This is based on the placement data available from your open Amrita placement tab and publicly reported recruiter trends.

  • Cognizant (CTS)
    • Cognizant is one of the largest recruiters at Amrita every year. They usually offer roles under GenC, GenC Pro, and occasionally GenC Next.
    • Highest Package at Amrita: Around 6–7 LPA for GenC Pro roles.
    • Common Roles:
      • GenC Programmer
      • GenC Pro Developer
      • Digital Engineer
    • Placement Trend:
      • CTS hires in large numbers, especially for CSE, ECE, and EEE students. Their tests focus on aptitude + basic coding.

  • Tech Mahindra
    • Tech Mahindra is a consistent recruiter across Amrita campuses.
    • Highest Package at Amrita: Typically 4–5 LPA.
    • Common Roles:
      • Associate Software Engineer
      • Associate Software Engineer
      • Support Engineer
    • Placement Trend:
      • They hire for IT services and telecom-based software roles. Their process is moderate in difficulty.

  • Wipro
    • Wipro offers multiple hiring tracks like WILP, Turbo, and Elite
    • Highest Package at Amrita:
      Wipro Turbo: ~7–8 LPA
      Wipro Elite: ~3.5–4 LPA
    • Common Roles:
      • Project Engineer
      • Software Engineer
      • Cloud/Infra Support
    • Placement Trend:
      • Wipro is one of the top mass recruiters at Amrita. Turbo roles are offered to high performers.
