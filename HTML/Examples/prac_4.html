Fun Time Movie Description

MOVIE : HARRY POTTER AND SERIES

The Harry Potter film series consists of 8 movies released between 2001 and 2011, each adapting J.K. Rowling’s novels and following Harry’s journey from ch ildhood to the final battle against Voldemort. Alongside this, your open tabs are focused on Harry Potter images, wallpapers, and fan art resources.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone (2001)

The first film introduces us to Harry, an orphan who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he befriends Ron and Hermione. Together, they uncover the mystery of the Sorcerer’s Stone and face Voldemort’s attempt to return to power. This movie sets the magical tone of the series, full of wonder and discovery.




2.Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (2002)

In his second year, Harry hears strange voices and learns about t he legend of the Chamber of Secrets. Students a re mysteriously being petrified, and suspicion falls on Harry. With the help of his friends, he discovers the entrance to the Chamber, battles a giant basilisk, and learns more about Voldemort’s past through Tom Riddle’s diary




3. Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004)

Harry learns that Sirius Black, a dangerous escaped prisoner, is supposedly hunting him. Dementors guard Hogwarts, and Harry uncovers the truth about his parents’ past. With Hermione’s help and a Time-Turner, he saves Sirius and a Hippogriff named Buckbeak.




4. Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2005)

Harry is unexpectedly chosen for the dangerous Triwizard Tournament. He faces dragons, mermaids, and a deadly maze. The tournament ends with Voldemort’s return, marking the beginning of dark times.




5. Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (2007)

The Ministry denies Voldemort’s return, so Harry forms “Dumbledore’s Army” to train students. He faces visions connected to Voldemort and battles Death Eaters at the Ministry, losing Sirius Black in the process.




6. Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2009)

Harry discovers a mysterious potions book belonging to the “Half-Blood Prince.” Dumbledore shows him Voldemort’s past and the secret of Horcruxes. The film ends with Dumbledore’s tragic death at the Astronomy Tower.




7. Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1 (2010)

Harry, Ron, and Hermione leave Hogwarts to hunt Horcruxes. They face danger, betrayal, and the growing power of Voldemort. The movie ends with Dobby’s death and Voldemort stealing the Elder Wand.




8. Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 2 (2011)

The final battle erupts at Hogwarts. Harry learns he is a Horcrux and sacrifices himself. Voldemort is ultimately defeated, and peace returns to the wizarding world.
