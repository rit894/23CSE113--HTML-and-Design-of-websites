These schools offer more than 300 undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, making Amrita one of India’s largest multidisciplinary universities

RESEARCH PROJECTS

  • Enhancing teaching and learning of vocational skills using machine learning and cognitive training.
    • Impact: Empowered thousands of rural women by training them in trades like plumbing, tailoring, and many more.
    • Researchers: Led by Prof. Bhavani Rao and team at AMMACHI Labs.
  • Moral Reasoning & Language Skills Project
    • Impact: Helps secondary school students develop ethical decision-making and communication abilities.
    • Researchers: Faculty from the School of Social & Behavioural Sciences.
  • Mental Health Project – AAIR! Building Inner Strength
    • Impact: Addresses rising mental health challenges in India through resilience-building workshops.
    • Researchers: Psychology and social sciences faculty; funded by a private donor (₹10 lakh).
  • Computational Engineering & Networking (CEN) Projects
    • Impact: Innovations in speech recognition, computer vision, robotics, and embedded systems.
    • Researchers: Faculty from the Center for Computational Engineering and Networking (CEN), several ranked in Stanford’s Top 2% Scientists list.
  • Healthcare & Nanoscience Research
    • Impact: Breakthroughs in drug delivery systems, cancer research, and regenerative medicine.
    • Researchers: Faculty from the School of Nanosciences & Molecular Medicine, Kochi campus.
